Meet Duo Alinavot

Duo Alinavot is a dynamic hand-to-hand and clowning circus duo that brings laughter, wonder, and breathtaking acrobatics to audiences around the world. With a perfect blend of physical comedy and stunning aerial choreography, they create unforgettable performances that resonate with all ages.

Their unique artistic approach combines the precision of hand-to-hand acrobatics with the spontaneity and humor of clowning, creating a theatrical experience that is both technically impressive and deeply human. Each performance tells a story through movement, comedy, and genuine connection with the audience.

With backgrounds in contemporary circus, physical theater, and clowning traditions, the duo has performed at festivals and venues across Europe and beyond, constantly pushing the boundaries of what circus can be.

Our Show

A Journey of Balance and Joy

Duo Alinavot's signature show is an exhilarating journey that explores the delicate balance between strength and vulnerability, precision and chaos, partnership and individuality. Through stunning hand-to-hand acrobatics interwoven with moments of pure comedic brilliance, the duo invites audiences into their world of playful exploration and trust.

The performance seamlessly transitions between breathtaking acrobatic sequences and intimate clowning moments, creating a rich tapestry of emotion and spectacle. Every catch, every fall, every laugh is a testament to their years of training and their deep artistic partnership.
