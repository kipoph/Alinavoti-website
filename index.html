Meet Duo Alinavot

Duo Alinavot is a dynamic hand-to-hand and clowning circus duo that brings laughter, wonder, and breathtaking acrobatics to audiences around the world. With a blend of physical comedy and flowing acrobitc circus sequences, they create unforgettable performances that resonate with all ages.

Their unique artistic approach combines the precision of hand-to-hand acrobatics with the spontaneity and humor of clowning, creating a theatrical experience that is both technically impressive and deeply human. Each performance tells a story through movement, comedy, and genuine connection with the audience.
